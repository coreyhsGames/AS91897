ABOUT US


Our Promise

Here at Retro Candy, the mission is to bring the world of candy with just a click of a button. We aim to provide a delightful and diverse shopping experience for candy lovers of all ages and tastes. Whether you are a child trying out new flavours, a teenager seeking the latest candy trends, a parent looking for quality sweets for your family, a young adult indulging in nostalgic favourites, or an adult with a sophisticated appetite, we have something special for everyone.

History

Founded in the early 2000s, Retro Candy pays tribute to the vibrant and tasty sweets that defined the '80s and '90s. From classic chocolate bars to nostalgic novelties, the store quickly became a local favorite, blending quality treats. Today, Retro Candy continues to delight customers of all ages, offering a taste of nostalgia and a trip down memory lane with every visit.
